Seaweed Salad

Description

Seaweed Salad is a common cold dish before hot dishes being served in Chinese cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Dried Seaweed
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Sesame oil
  • Optional: sesame seeds, chili

Steps

  1. Soak the dried seaweed to full size
  2. Boil it in water for 3 minutes, rinse it and drain
  3. Mix well the seaweed with soy sauce, vinegar, sugar and sesame oil
  4. Add sesame seeds and chili to the top
  5. Enjoy!
