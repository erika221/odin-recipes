Steamed Eggs

Description

Steamed Eggs are a quick easy dish. Perfect to go with rice. Popular among children!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Crack a few eggs in a bowl
  2. Use a whisk or chopsticks to mix egg york and egg white well
  3. Use a spoon to remove the bubbles made from the mixing step
  4. Move it to a shallow bowl, cover the bowl with a plastic food wrap
  5. Steam it for 10 minutes
  6. When hot, add soy sauce and green onions
  7. Enjoy!
