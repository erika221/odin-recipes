Bubble Tea

Description

Bubbletea is a sweet drink with chewy tapiocas. This is an easy fast receipt for making 500ml original flavoured bubble tea.

Ingredients

  • Instant Tapioca
  • Black Tea Bags
  • White/Brown Sugar
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Boil 500ml water in a small pot
  2. When boiling, turn to low heat, add the tea bags
  3. Boil for 5 minutes, remove the pot off the heat
  4. Add sugar and milk in
  5. Boil 1000ml water in a different pot
  6. When boiling, add instant tapioca
  7. Stir to avoid stick to the bottom
  8. Boil for 5 minutes, then remove from the heat
  9. Put on a lid and let it sit for 2 minutes
  10. Drain the tapioca, and rinse under the tap water to wash off the starch
  11. Pour the milktea in a cup, add tapioca and ice
  12. Enjoy!
